Removing barriers of entry to blockchain development.

It's time for us to make meaningful comparisons among layer 1 blockchain technologies by learning to build on them yourself. We're starting with a collection of small, well known contracts which we will build using multiple technologies.

Together we can build small and focused demos to help developers of every level quickly orient themselves to web3

How does this work?

Presenters register for a demo

Everyone is welcome to present their favorite layer 1
solution. Every event shows a working technical demo.

Everyone gathers for Demo Day

Events last 45 minutes from start to finish including Q&A. Demos are limited to 25 minutes.

The community selects a topic.

The web3exp community members decide on the next topic. Presenters choose any layer 1 technology for the demo but must focus on the selected topic.

The event date is announced

All demos are at 6a PST / 9a EST / 5p CET / 7:30p IST. Demos are recorded and made available online.
